Complete Rank Progression System

The system is split into two phases: Level Progression and the elite Legend Master Ranks.

Phase 1: Rank Level Progression (27 Levels)

This phase uses a Rank Score system and standard matchmaking. The ranks are categorized into tiers, with 5 divisions each (V is lowest, I is highest).

Rank Tier Divisions (Lowest → Highest) Total Levels
1. Rookie V → IV → III → II → I 5
2. Silver Racer V → IV → III → II → I 5
3. Gold Racer V → IV → III → II → I 5
4. Diamond Racer V → IV → III → II → I 5
5. Elite V → IV → III → II → I 5
6. Master V → IV 2
7. Super Master (The 27th Level) 1

Phase 2: Legend Master Rank Progression

After Super Master, players enter the Legend tier, which uses a geography-based progression and a unique Challenge Matchmaking system.

1. Legend (Regional)

Achieve mastery in your region and hold your rank for the season.

2. Legend (National)

Progress from Regional, but reset to Bronze V (National) to prove your worth again.

3. Legend (Continent)

Progress from National, resetting to Bronze V (Continent).

4. Legend (Global)

Progress from Continent, resetting to Bronze V (Global).

5. Super Lightning

The absolute peak of the Global rank. The highest achievable status.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Welcome to Project Racer Sim Hub. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This privacy policy sets out how we collect, use, and protect any information that you give us when you use this website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password. This is stored securely via Authentication.
  • Profile & Gameplay Data: We fetch and store your driver profile information, including your license, rank, club affiliations, and gameplay statistics. This data is linked to your Project Racer account to provide the core experience of the Sim Hub.
  • User-Generated Content: Any content you create, such as club announcements or chat messages, is stored and associated with your profile.
  • Technical Data: We use your browser's local time to display clocks correctly. With your consent, we may also use your geolocation to provide more accurate timezone-related features. This is not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and maintain our platform, including to:

  • Authenticate you and provide access to your driver dashboard.
  • Display your rank, stats, and club information.
  • Enable communication features like club chat and announcements.
  • Administer championships and display standings.
  • Provide a personalized experience, such as showing correct local and event times.

3. Data Sharing and Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Firestore, Firebase Authentication). We do not sell, distribute, or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so. Your driver name, rank, and club tag are publicly visible within the Sim Hub to facilitate competition.

4. Your Rights & Control

You have control over your personal data. You can review and update your profile information at any time through your dashboard. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact our support team. Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

5. Data Consent

Our website requires the use of necessary data (such as your local time) to function correctly. By using the site, you consent to this necessary data processing. The consent banner will be displayed on your first visit and can be dismissed. Dismissal is stored locally in your browser.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@lemansmobile.com.
